About Me

I am a passionate ML Engineer and AI Researcher specializing in LLM applications, Trustworthy AI, Computer Vision, and Multimodality. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Business Systems, I am actively involved in cutting-edge research at the Indian Statistical Institute and have industry experience as a Machine Learning Engineer at Geogo Techsolutions.

My research interests include applications of Large Language Models for Medical Report Generation, hallucination detection in LLMs, prompt engineering, and computer vision models. I have publications accepted at prestigious conferences like AAAI and work under review at IJCNN.
